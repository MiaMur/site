Amber Whatts

Every day I'm thinking about change. I want people to be afraid of the women I style. I like things simple. You have a more interesting life if you wear impressive hairstyles. My aim is to make the poor look rich and the rich look poor.

My learning process is by eye alone; it's not at all scientific. Fashions fade, style is eternal. The only way to do something in depth is to work hard. Success isn't always about the end result, it's about what you learn along the way. I do not have one cut for each season, I just try to make beautiful hair all year round.
